About Us

Ingress is an augmented reality game that turns the world around you into a battle,
with humankind's minds at stake.
Play Ingress on the streets of Victoria. Meet agents who play in the areas you do and
fight with them to protect humanity's freedom. Discover agents you can depend on
to help you when you need assistance in the field.
Download the mobile scanner app to discover thousands of portals around you.
Buildings, historic landmarks and cultural points of interest become portals for you
to fight for and defend from alien incursion.

The Victorian Resistance has a long and proud history of teamwork, collaboration
and most importantly, fun!
By joining us, you'll have access to regular events so you can stock up on gear,
regular operations so you can put your mark on the map, as well as help almost any
time of the day or night.
